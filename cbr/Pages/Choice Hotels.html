Enjoy preferred rates at over 7,000 properties worldwide for work and play.

Choice Hotels® includes Comfort™, Quality®, Clarion® and others like the upscale Ascend Hotel Collection®. There are over 330 Choice locations across Canada - and over 7,000 worldwide. Hotels are conveniently located near major airports, key highways, and business districts. You’ll also be able to connect easily with colleagues and friends using free high-speed Internet, and jump-start your day with free breakfast at most locations.

How to book Preferred Rates for Cannonball Rides

  • DESKTOP: Click here for direct access to Prefrred Rates online or visit ChoiceHotels.com and from the drop down rate menu enter your Rate ID 00332040 in the Special Rate/Corporate ID box before you search for locations.

  • MOBILE APP: Download the Choice Hotels app and enter your Special Rate/Corporate ID 00332040 to the Company Information section of your online profile: My Account > Update Profile > Company Information.

  • CALL CENTRE: Call 800.424.6423 (800.4CHOICE) and provide the call agent with your Special Rate/Corporate ID 00332040 before requesting location and dates.

Virtual Pay: Bring efficiency and security to your business travel.

The Virtual Pay Program allows you to book and pay for hotel rooms centrlly, even for travellers without a corporate card, making it easy for both the traveller and Travel Manager. To find out more or to sign up contact us at ClientServices@ChoiceHotels.ca
