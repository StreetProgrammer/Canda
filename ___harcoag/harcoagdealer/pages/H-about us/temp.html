ABOUT HARCO AG EQUIPMENT

Posted: October 1, 2020

Business was started in 1977 by Jim Harkness selling new and used equipment to which wholesale lines were gradually added.

In September 1982 the company was incorporated as Jim Harkness Equipment Ltd., HARCO Ag Equipment was registered as a trade name of the wholesale division.

HARCO Ag Equipment is located on Highway #9 between Harriston and Clifford, Ontario. 50 miles north of Highway #401, Ontario's main east-west artery. We are 1 1/2 hours northwest of Ontario's largest city Toronto, 1 hour north of major centres Guelph and Kitchener, 3 1/2 hours from Windsor-Detroit and 2 1/2 hours from Sarnia-Port Huron which are our two major border crossing points.

In 2002 a storage and drop off area was established north of Woodstock, Ontario approximately 12 miles from Highway #401.

The company sells to Farm Equipment, Rental, Turf and other businesses in Ontario with approximately 300 dealers, in Quebec with approximately 120 dealers and with a total of 100+ dealers throughout New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, Prince Edward Island and Newfoundland.

We also operate HarcoTrans which includes three highway tractors with 53' step deck trailers, one tractor with 53' vinyl slider flat bed and one 'tilt n load' for local deliveries. Our main concentration includes Northern Ontario, Quebec and the Maritimes. Call Wes Hope for more information & very competitive pricing.
