WHY CHOOSE US

 

Locally Owned and Operated

Northshore Sports and Auto Inc. began in 2008 as a used automotive and recreational vehicle dealer located in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario.  We are excited to announce that in December 2013 we also officially became a licensed BRP dealership and are located 647 MacDonald Ave. Sault Ste Marie ON P6B 1J4 .

 

Our experience

As a family owned and operated business, we understand the importance of quality service and products and we work hard to ensure that all customers have a positive experience before and after each and every sale.  We are committed to providing the best products at the best prices!

 

The Brands

BRP has been, and continues to be, a world leader in the industry of motorized recreational vehicles and powersport engines for over 70 years.  We are proud to say that we carry, support and service Ski-Doo snowmobiles, Sea-Doo watercraft and boats and Can-Am all-terrain and side-by-side vehicles.  We also have a variety of used automotive and recreational vehicles.

 

The selection

Please take a moment and browse our online new and pre-owned inventory and if you have any questions or would like further information - e-mail, call or come see us!
