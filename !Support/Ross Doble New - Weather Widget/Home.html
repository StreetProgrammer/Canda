OUR COMPANYS HISTORY

Ross Doble inc. is located in the Heart of Central Ontario.  We are a large modern dealer facility that sells Kubota, Kuhn, Krause, Hardi and Land Pride, as well as a large amount of short lines to ease your day to day rural living.  Family owned for over 70 years.  We see all makes, models and sizes - from combines to lawnmowers thru our Service doors because of our experience and low door rate. 
 
Sales staff listens, evaluates and recommends what fits your needs best with no pressure tactics.  Our Parts staff have over 40 years experience, they know the new and old.  The Office Manager will be more than happy to open an account for you, accepting Visa, Mastercard Credit Cards.  We are your dealer.
