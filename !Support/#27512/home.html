Welcome to Richards Equipment Inc.
Richards Equipment Inc. is a company rich in history and tradition. We have served the agricultural and construction industries of York Region and Simcoe County for over 50 years.

Richards Equipment Inc. is an authorized New Holland, Mahindra and Cub Cadet dealership specializing in the sale and service of agricultural, compact tractor, light construction and lawn and garden equipment. We are dedicated to serving our local community in Barrie, Ontario.

We strive to provide the best experience possible for your equipment needs. Stop by or contact us today and we can help determine an equipment solution that works best for you and your operation.
