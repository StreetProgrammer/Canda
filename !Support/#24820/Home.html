    At Dan R Equipment we pride ourselves on our customer focus. We want your business to succeed.
    Give us a call or stop by to experience the Dan R difference.
     

    Dan R Equipment is your eastern Ontario equipment solution. As a quality conscious organization we only sell products we have confidence and pride in, and we stand behind our customers.

    We provide sales, service and parts for a full line of AGCO products, including top tier tractor brands such as Massey Ferguson, Challenger and Fendt. When it comes to harvest we are the home of the Gleaner and Dion brands. Sunflower tillage and White Planters. From spring to fall, tillage to harvest, we have you covered.

    For the landscape professional and acreage owner, we carry Echo, and Bearcat products as well as Woods mowers and implements. For your entire commercial, residential and landscaping requirements, we have the right equipment for the job.

    We pride ourselves on customer service and we want to earn your business. Give us a call or stop by to experience the Dan R Equipment difference.
