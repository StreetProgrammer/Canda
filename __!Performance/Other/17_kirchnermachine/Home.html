    Kirchner Machine Ltd

    Kirchner Machine is a third generation, family run business with over 70 years of experience building farm equipment.  It began when two brothers, Harold and Earl Kirchner first moved to Southern Alberta and discovered the land’s heavy weed problem.  They began growing Alfalfa to combat it, and soon realized that quality hay equipment was hard to find.  So they built it themselves…and thus, a company was born.
