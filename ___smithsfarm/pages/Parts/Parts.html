Our Parts Department: 

Smiths Farm Equipment maintains a large inventory of quality OEM and aftermarket parts. Smiths carries parts for the complete New Holland Agriculture line including all horse-power tractors, hay and forage, material handling and skid steers.  Smiths also carries parts for a variety of snow blowers, lawn and garden, finishing mowers, rotary cutters, rakes and tedders. Also able to source parts from aftermarket suppliers for competitive brands – John Deere, Massey Fergusson, Case IH, Gehl, Claas, Vermeer, Overum, and many others. Call our parts department to see how they can help you!

Hydraulic hoses made while you wait! Smiths carries Gates hydraulic hose and numerous fittings including British, Pipe, JIC and o-ring boss!

Having difficulty keeping your livestock penned? Smiths carries all different sizes of Diamond Bar gates and Red Brand fencing supplies.  

Need a gift idea for that one relative that’s difficult to buy for?  Smiths carries a large variety of ERTL New Holland toys, not only for the kids but for the kid at heart!  Die-cast and plastic models available!  New Holland branded knives, hand tools, gloves, grease guns, filter wrenches, hats, traveler mugs, shop clocks, outside thermometers, belt buckles, metal signs and lots more!

Winter means cold weather, salt and sand! Smiths carries multiple options for fuel conditioner, including Stanadyne, DSG and Kleen-Flo.  Protect against rust with our Krown products, carried all year around.  Winter also means shorter days, so stop by Smiths and check out our LED lighting options! 

Need a new chain?  Smiths carries OEM New Holland AMERICAN MADE chain as well as off-shore chain in a variety of sizes, including heavy duty and double.  While picking up a new chain, check out our Krown chain lubricant! 

Chainsaw Oil for $9.95!  Light, medium and heavy available all year!  Lots of chainsaw accessories available including chain, bars, files, gloves and more! 

Smiths Parts Department has something to offer everyone, from the cash-crop farmer who relies on us to get him back up and going quickly to the homeowner who needs to get his lawn mower running to keep his lawn pristine.  Stop in and see how we can make your day!
