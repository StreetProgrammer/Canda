Welcome to Bobs Motorsport Ltd, a locally owned and operated powersport dealer, proudly serving the Chatham-Kent and surrounding area since 1977.

We are your trusted dealer for Yamaha, Suzuki and ARGO, offering a wide selection of motorcycles, ATVs, side by sides, snowmobiles, KingQuads, and amphibious 6- and 8-wheel extreme terrain vehicles.

Stocked with a variety of quality parts and accessories including jackets, helmets, gloves and gear, we have you covered, while our service department, led by trained technicians, are equipped to service all makes and models.

Built on a reputation for providing exceptional customer service, we are committed to ensuring you the quality of service you’ve come to expect at Bobs Motorsport. Visit our showroom or speak to one of our representatives for all your powersports needs.
