WHAT DOES IT MEAN TO BE ELITE?

To be among the Elite is to be among the best of the best. Through the Elite Dealer Program, Yamaha recognizes those dealers who are committed to going above and beyond for their customers to make sure they have the best experience possible.
 
The Elite Dealer program builds on the foundation of the former Five Star program in making sure your customer experience is one that cant be beat, from the moment you walk into the showroom to when you pick up your keys to your new Yamaha.
 
In order to qualify for Elite status, Yamaha dealers must apply for and undergo a thorough evaluation process to ensure that they exceed the national average in a variety of areas including:
 
  • Clean, professional showrooms
  • Excellent customer service
  • Knowledgeable technicians
  • Customer satisfaction
Only a select number of dealers from all over Canada have achieved the prestigious Elite Dealer status. In order to retain their status, dealers must apply and successfully meet the requirements every year, as determined by Yamaha Motor Canada. Criteria is updated and adjusted accordingly to reflect current market conditions.
