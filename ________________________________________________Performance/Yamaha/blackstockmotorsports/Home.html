Welcome to
BLACKSTOCK MOTORSPORTS
In the summer of 2017 Jim & Josh Michaud acquired Asselstine Country with the idea of continuing the longstanding business while adding something new. Blackstock Motorsports was that something. New name, same family focus, same great location with the Yamaha and Suzuki brands riders love.

Our motto is "We sell fun!" and we think there's a number of things that go into making this true. A straightforward sales experience, our mechanic's expertise and knowledgeable parts & service staff. Our commitment is to ensure you, our customer, have a positive experience no matter whether you're looking for a new or new to you machine, riding gear & accessories, having routine service performed or are facing a technical difficulty that needs some creative solutions. Our hope is that we can help you get back to what we truly support....Fun!
