DURHAM KUBOTA

Durham Kubota, Oshawa, Ontario is focused on ensuring all customers receive exceptional service for all your Agriculture and equipment needs. At Durham Kubota we have the latest diagnostic tools and repair techniques, authorized and approved by Kubota. Call us and see the difference for yourself.

We are authorized dealers for Kubota, STIHL, Ariens, Gravely, LandPride, Reist Industries and Wallenstein. We are also dealers for Walco, Harco Ag and Farm Fleet products.

We have a long history of providing homeowners, tradesmen, agriculturists and industries with a variety of products to help them in their construction projects, cultivating tasks and exterior maintenance.

Trust Walter & Leanne Steffler, the new owners of Durham Kubota, to supply you with top quality equipment for your works. Having worked for 16 years with Kubota Canada Ltd, Walter has gained the knowledge and expertise to be able to provide the best advice and products to people in all fields of activity.
