Why We Sell Canadian-Made Trailers?

When we opened, we decided we only wanted to offer the highest quality trailers on the market. After reviewing dozens of different trailer types and brands, it became obvious that Canadian-Made Trailers where the way to go. Why? They are built tough and designed for our Canadian climate. Many of the trailers manufactured in the USA simply arent built for our tough winter temperatures, the ice, the salt that we as Canadians experience. Plus, we want to provide the absolute most value to our clients and with trailers not designed for this country, combine with the US exchange rate, it made Canadian products the obvious choice.
