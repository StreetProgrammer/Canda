Dewildt Marine & Powersports

DeWildt Marine & Powersports now has 3 dealerships located in Hamilton, Innisfil and Peterborough. We are proud to represent industry leading brands including Honda, Yamaha, Crestliner, Legend, Starcraft, Aquasport and Mercury Marine. All of our locations offer fully staffed sales, parts, accessories and service departments as well as winter storage. We look forward to helping you get out on the water, the trails or on the roadwherever your passion for the outdoors may take you!
