A Word About The Risk

Motorcycling is inherently a risky sport - it is your responsibility to stay safe.

Our courses are fun but also strenuous, tiring, and mentally and physically challenging.

Its up to you to speak up anytime during your course to opt out if you have reached your limit or are not comfortable.

Who we Are

Our founder and director, Toni Sharpless is an accomplished motorcycle racer who has spent decades racing motorcycles in North America and around the world, landing her a spot in the Canadian Motorcycle Hall of Fame. After seeing the training and support that exists throughout Europe and Asia for young riders, Toni wants to bring some of that home to Canada.

Currently in Canada you are eligible to race in CSBK, the national road racing series when you reach the age of 15. With limited ways for kids to practice on pavement, our motorcycle athletes have been dropping well behind the world leaders in road racing. Not since Canadians Michelle Duff in the 60s and Miguel Duhamel in the 90s has Canada had a rider on the podium at world series road race event.

Our Mission

Our mission at the Super Sonic Road Racing School is to provide a fun and safe introduction to road racing motorcycles for all ages and experience levels with a clear aim to give rise to future Canadian Champions competing internationally at the highest level.

Toni Sharpless
