ACCESSORY WARRANTY

Mitsubishi Motors Genuine Accessories installed by an Authorized Mitsubishi Motors Dealer are considered for warranty purposes to be original equipment and covered by the MMSCAN New Vehicle Limited Warranty.

GENUINE ACCESSORIES

The following factory, port or Authorized Mitsubishi Motors dealer-installed Mitsubishi Motors Genuine Accessories are covered for 3 years or 60,000 km, whichever comes first, excluding wear and tear.

OVER THE COUNTER PARTS AND ACCESSORIES WARRANTY

Mitsubishi Authorized Parts and Accessories sold “over the counter” to parts wholesalers or other retailers are warranted against defects in material or workmanship for 12 months or 20,000 odometer kilometres, whichever occurs first, from date of installation. For an example of how to submit warranty claims on failed parts (or accessories) sold over the counter, see the Warranty Claim Entry Guide. Evidence of the original purchase must be retained in dealership files. Under the terms of the Parts and Accessory warranty, labour, towing to the nearest dealer, or collateral damage to the vehicle, may only be claimed if the failed part was installed by a Mitsubishi dealer.

INSTALLATION

Installation at an Authorized Mitsubishi Motors Dealer can be done anytime within the MMSCAN New Vehicle Limited Warranty period to receive coverage for the full basic warranty period. Installations completed at non-Authorized Mitsubishi Motors Dealers are covered under the Parts Warranty for parts only for 12 months or 20,000 kilometres, whichever occurs first.

DEALER-INSTALLED PARTS AND ACCESSORIES INSTALLED ON MITSUBISHI PRODUCTS AFTER DELIVERY

Mitsubishi Authorized Parts and Accessories installed on Mitsubishi products after delivery are warranted against defects in material or workmanship, for the unexpired term of the New Vehicle Limited Warranty or if more favourable, for the Parts and Accessories Warranty of 12 months or 20,000 odometer kilometres from the date of installation, whichever occurs first. Under the terms of Mitsubishi’s Parts and Accessory warranty, labour, towing to the nearest Mitsubishi dealer, or collateral damage to the vehicle, may only be claimed if a Mitsubishi dealer installed the failed part.

*Whichever comes first. Regular maintenance not included. New Vehicle Limited Warranty covers most vehicle parts (excluding batteries, with entertainment systems and other items excluded under the warranty's terms and conditions) under normal use and maintenance. DIAMOND CARE MECHANICAL BREAKDOWN PROTECTION

Diamond Care Mechanical Breakdown Protection (MBP) offers comfort in knowing your vehicle is protected should an unexpected covered breakdown occur. Simply take your vehicle to any Mitsubishi dealership in Canada or the continental United States, and our skilled Mitsubishi technicians will handle the rest. With two comprehensive coverage levels, several term lengths and deductible options, Diamond Care MBP can be customized to meet your needs.

Diamond Care MBP is also packed with additional features such as 24-hour roadside assistance, trip interruption benefit, car rental benefit, and a claim-free reward benefit.1 With Diamond Care MBP, experience peace of mind on every journey.

DIAMOND CARE LOAN PROTECTION

Diamond Care Loan Protection3 offers Life, Critical Illness, Loss of Employment and Disability coverage that protects you and your loved ones in the event of a life-altering event, illness or death. This valuable coverage ensures your financial commitments are met at a time when it’s needed most.

By making your monthly vehicle finance payment, Loan Protection provides the comfort of knowing your credit rating is protected and your vehicle will stay with the family where it belongs.

DIAMOND CARE APPEARANCE PROTECTION

Everyone loves that new car look and smell – the pristine exterior, the sheen of the wheels and the perfectly clean interior. With Diamond Care Appearance Protection, you could experience the new car feeling for years to come.

Appearance Protection is an interior and exterior coverage plan2 designed to take the worry out of life’s little mishaps. There’s nothing worse than your first door ding at the shopping centre, a windshield chip or a coffee spill. Appearance Protection alleviates the cost of these little incidents, keeping your vehicle looking its best and maximizing its resale value.

Depending on the plan chosen, coverage may include Interior Protection, Paintless Dent Removal, Paint Repair, Key and Keyless Remote Replacement, Car Rental Benefit, Front Windshield Protection, Head/Tail Light Protection, Tire and Wheel Protection and Curb Scuff Repair.

DIAMOND CARE VEHICLE LOSS PRIVILEGE PROGRAM

Diamond Care Vehicle Loss Privilege Program4 provides assistance in the event of a vehicle loss due to a collision, fire or theft, with a credit that you may use towards a replacement vehicle at your Mitsubishi dealer.

With privileges that protect you from Negative Equity, Total Loss, Theft Event and Partial Loss, rest assured that you remain protected against high replacement costs.
