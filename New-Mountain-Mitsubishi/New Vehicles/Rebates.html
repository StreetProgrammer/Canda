LOYALTY REBATE

IT’S THE REWARD FOR OUR VERY BEST CUSTOMERS BECAUSE, WELL, WE REALLY, REALLY LIKE YOU.
In fact, we like you so much we’re offering a loyalty rebate in addition to great financing and leasing programs! We wouldn’t offer this to just anyone but, like we said, we really, really like you. See your local dealer for more details

FIRST RESPONDERS PROGRAM

TO SERVE AND PROTECT.
At Mitsubishi, we value those who protect and serve our communities. To say thank you, a rebate is available for active Canadian First Responders – Fire Fighters, Emergency Medical Services (EMS) and Police Officers – to help make purchasing most new Mitsubishi vehicles easier. Visit your local dealer for more details.

EDUCATION EDGE

WHEN SCHOOL ENDS IT DOESN'T MEAN THE CHALLENGES END AS WELL.
That's why, in addition to other great financing programs at the best available rates, Mitsubishi Motors is also offering grads and soon-to-be grads an extra reward: a grad rebate. No matter what kind of school you're a graduate of: a university, an accredited college, a military academy, a nursing school or a trade school. See your local dealer for details.

MILITARY PROGRAM

THIS IS OUR WAY OF SAYING THANK YOU.
For your service, we’re at your service. For those who do the most, we have a little something to make purchasing a Mitsubishi even easier. In appreciation for your service, a military rebate is available with the purchase of select new Mitsubishi vehicles for current members of the Canadian Armed Forces and veterans. See your local dealer for details.

MOBILITY PROGRAM

SAFETY. RELIABILITY. ACCESSIBILITY.
Getting into a Mitsubishi is easier than ever. And now so is getting out of one. Now you can get a Mobility Rebate towards converting a Mitsubishi with driver/passenger assistance equipment. See your dealer for more details.
