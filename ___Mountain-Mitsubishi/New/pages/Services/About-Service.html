HOURS OF OPERATION

In the unlikely event of a breakdown or similar roadside emergency, you also have access to concierge services 24 hours a day, 365 days a year while traveling throughout Canada.

Concierge Service covers the following:

We will assist you with breakdowns, lockouts, flat tires and even deliver fuel for you.

We will arrange a rental vehicle for you through car rental agencies in your area.*

In the event of a delay associated with a breakdown, we will arrange and/or change airline reservations.*

Overnight accommodations can be made for you at the hotel or motel of your choice, or existing reservations can be changed as needed due to delays.*

A representative will notify your family members or colleagues to inform them of you delay, call a business associate to cancel a meeting, or take care of any other necessary details to help ease the strain of the delay.

We will locate the nearest ATM using your specific network system (Star, Plus Cirrus, etc.), according to the area code and prefix from which you call.

Schedule your appointment at Mountain  Mitsubishi today; we look forward to exceeding your service expectations!
