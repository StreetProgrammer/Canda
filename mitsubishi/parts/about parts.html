Parts Department

Why use OEM parts?

  • All OEM parts are manufactured to strict Mitsubishi standards.

  • It's easier to choose your part when you don't have to worry about assessing the quality of different brands and prices.

  • Consistency: the OEM part should work exactly as the one you are replacing.

  • Warranty

  • Aftermarket parts affect market value: If you lease your car, there are also economic considerations. Since aftermarket parts decrease a vehicle's book value, using them to repair your vehicle's body may cost you part or all of your security deposit.

Accessory Warranty

Mitsubishi Motors Genuine Accessories installed by an Authorized Mitsubishi Motors Dealer are considered for warranty purposes to be original equipment and covered by the MMSCAN New Vehicle Limited Warranty.
