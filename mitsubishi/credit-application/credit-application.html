Credit Application

We understand that purchasing a new or used vehicle can at times be a daunting process, luckily our team at Cambridge Mitsubishi are experts in 2nd, 3rd and 4th time credit application. By hosting our own financing in-house we are able to assist our customers with rebuilding credit while providing optimum finance and lease rates over an mutually agreed upon timeline. If you're a student applying for the first time or you've stumbled with credit in the past, we look forward to assisting you in making the application process a smooth one.

Before we begin, start by completing our online credit application form below. The form is easy to use and is the first step in becoming pre-approved for your next vehicle purchase. By filling out the form, you are providing our team with the basic information required to begin the credit application process. Once you've submitted your form, one of our financial experts will be in touch to obtain additional details necessary to complete your application. We do this so that there's no worry of anyone analyzing your credit score without your prior full disclosure.

It's as simple as that, credit application made easy. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us or give us a call, we look forward to helping you.

As always, we respect your privacy and can ensure you that all data submitted on the form below is strictly confidential and is not sold to 3rd party companies.
