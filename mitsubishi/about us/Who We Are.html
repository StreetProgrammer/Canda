Our Mission

Our mission at Cambridge Mitsubishi is to provide you, our customer, with the best product and the best automotive value. As trained sales professionals, we promise to provide prompt, precise service in a family-friendly atmosphere. We wish to exceed your expectations and earn your business for life.
Come visit us in person at 2211 Eagle Street North, Cambridge, Ontario.
You can also call us at (519) 650-0609.
Cambridge Mitsubishi is pleased to represent Mitsubishi Canada in Cambridge, south side of Kitchener, Preston, Barber's Beach, Breslau, Brantford, Etonia, Falkland, Guelph, New Hamburg, Woodstock, Innerkip, Stratford, Aberfoyle, Glen Morris, Morriston, Princeton, Roseville, Puslinch, Drumbo, Branchton, Ayr, Saint George, and Paris, in Ontario.
The philosophy of the management at Cambridge Mitsubishi is that the customer is best and most completely served by the dealership in their area. We are committed with great passion to pamper those individuals who have chosen to make their Mitsubishi investment with us.

In addition to both new and used Mitsubishi Vehicles, Cambridge Mitsubishi offers a wide variety of premium domestic and import used vehicles such as Hyundai, Kia, Dodge, Nissan, Honda, Chrysler, Ford, Toyota, and Volkswagen! Our certified pre-owned vehicles come with a CARFAX report and a thorough 160-point inspection.

Take a look at the extensive list of genuine Mitsubishi parts & accessories offered in our Parts Department. Just like our vehicles, Mitsubishi accessories come with extended warranty! We have parts for every new Mitsubishi models, as well as many parts from prior years.

In addition to our Rebates program, we offer monthly specials on both pre-owned and new vehicles. Come take a look often as the specials are updated frequently! Every rebates are added on top of our current promotion, to maximize your savings! Still haven't found a car that suits your needs and your wallet? We offer great savings on demo models!
