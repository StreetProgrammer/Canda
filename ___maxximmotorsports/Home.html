WELCOME TO MAXXIM MOTORSPORTS GROUP

OAKVILLE ONTARIO

CFMOTO award winning all-terrain vehicles have the power to conquer, to go further and experience more. Explore rocky creek beds, colossal mud pits, wooded mountainsides, bush and farmer fields. Our lineup of high performance CFMOTO vehicles rip through the toughest terrain with ease.

If work and play are in your day, CFMOTO has a model that's right for you. Choose from the hard working and fun UFORCE UTVs, the agile and multipurpose CFORCE ATVs and the sporty and exhilarating ZFORCE SIDE x SIDE models. The lineup is built to the highest standards of quality and durability for the Canadian market. Dare to compare the "ALL IN AT NO EXTRA COST" factory integrated features of a CFMOTO and they will exceed your expectations.

Maxxim Motorsports Inc is proud to be your Authorized CFMOTO Dealer. Our knowledgeable staff welcomes you to our family and will ensure a great riding experience and a ton of fun. Come see us today!
