Turple Bros Ltd.

Turple Bros. Ltd. officially opened in Red Deer in 1956. It is still family owned and operated, with its passionate founder, Glenn Turple, leading the way. What started as the dream of two brothers has turned into one of the most well-known powersports dealerships in the area. We offer sales, service, accessories, parts, and financing on a wide selection of new and used motorcycles, ATVs, UTVs, personal watercrafts, and snowmobiles. We carry the latest and greatest from your favorite brands such as Can-Am, Honda, Suzuki, KTM, and more! We even offer power equipment!

When you visit Turple Bros. Ltd., you can feel confident that you will be given word-class customer service by experts in the industry. We strive for excellence and value each person who walks through our doors. Fairness and honesty have been important to the growth of Turple Bros. says Glenn, These should be key ingredients for any business. Stop by today and be a part of the Turple Bros. legacy!
