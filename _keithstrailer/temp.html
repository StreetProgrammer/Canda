Inglis Cycle Center

London, Ontario

Inglis Cycle Center is an authorized Yamaha, Suzuki, Triumph, CF Moto and Kawasaki dealership. We carry a large selection of new inventory as well as pre-owned inventory and have a full sales, service, and parts department with friendly and experienced staff. Visit or call us Monday through Saturday for all your powersports needs!

Inglis Cycle Center is conveniently located near the areas of London, Windsor, Kitchener/Waterloo, Cambridge, and Sarnia.
