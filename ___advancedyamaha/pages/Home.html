Company Overview

Advanced Motorsports Ltd was started in April 2004 by current owners Larry Parker and Dean Parker . We began as a small shop offering parts, accessories, and servicing Artic Cat, Honda, Kawasaki, Polaris, Suzuki, specializing in Yamaha.

We are a small family business that prides itself on honesty, integrity and personal service. In closing, our friendly knowledgeable staff is available at all times to meet your sales or service needs.

Advanced Motorsports has a great selection of Pre owned ATV, Motorcycle, and Snowmobiles. Whether you are an entry level rider or seasoned veteran, Advanced Motorsports provides quality products, expert knowledge, with factory trained technicians.

On-site test facilities for you to try our quality Pre owned inventory. In-house financing available OAC. No high pressure sales, gimmicks or hidden fees. Its worth the drive to Advanced Motorsports - the areas number one servicing dealer, just 15 minutes south of St. Thomas on Dexter Line.

We are the areas number one servicing dealer because:

At Advanced Motorsports we realize that seasons are short and available riding time is limited. We try our best to get you back to what you love doing as quickly as possible, since we want to see our customers riding, not waiting on repairs.
