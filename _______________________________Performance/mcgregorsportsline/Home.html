WELCOME TO McGregor Sportsline

McGregor Sporstline, Yamaha sales and service is located in the village of Bobcaygeon, Ontario on Highway 36. Specializing in Yamaha YZ and Dirt Bikes, ATV's, Snowmobiles and Generators, McGregor Sportsline established it's solid reputation one customer at a time.

McGregor Sportsline is a family owned business which has had 25 succesful years in Yamaha sales and service thanks to all our valued customers. Any one on our team would be pleased to discuss your recreational needs, whether it's a Yamaha ATV, or a Yamaha Snowmobile, our friendly staff is here to help. The business is known for it's excellent service department and customer service. In fact, the dealership has been the recipient of Yamaha Canada's Customer Satisfaction Award for the past 10 consecutive years.

Although McGregor Sportsline is small in size, it is mighty in happy customers from all parts of Canada. As an authorized dealer for Yamaha, Jonsered power equipment and Columbia lawn and garden equipment, count on McGregor Sportsline the next time you need to fulfill your need for a new Yamaha toy. Financing is available for new and used products up to 7 years old - OAC.
