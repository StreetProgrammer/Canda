Welcome

Tweed Recreational Sport & Lawn is an authorized YAMAHA Dealership located in Tweed, Ontario.

Our YAMAHA Team of Kirk, Julie, Rick and Gerrit welcomes you to browse their SHOWROOM. You will be delighted with the large selection of ATVs, Side by Sides, Avalon Pontoon Boats, Alumacraft Aluminum Boats, Yamaha Motors, Snowmobiles, Generators, Clothing, parts, accessories and more!

We are also a qualified retailer of HUSQVARNA power equipment, including chain saws, generators, power cutters, inverters and forestry clearing saws. For all of your seasonal needs, choose from the wide variety of HUSQVARNA tractors & mowers, tillers, blowers, trimmers, brushcutters and snowblowers. We carry everything you need to get the job done!
