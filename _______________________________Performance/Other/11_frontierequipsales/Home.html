Frontier Sales & Equipment

Frontier Sales Equipment is Wallenstein Ontario's authorized dealer for Wacker Neuson, Weidemann, Samasz, Picus, Hustler Turf, HLA and AMI Attachments. We can get the parts you need for your construction equipment. Unique parts too. Our service department can help get your industrial machinery working again and fast. We fix all the equipment OEMs we sell. If you are looking for wheeled loaders, backhoes, skid-steers, bucket attachments or grapples, we have the products for your excavation and construction jobs. Heavy lifting, bulldozing, snowplowing or yard care, we cover it all.
