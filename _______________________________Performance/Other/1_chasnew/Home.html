WELCOME TO Chas Richards & Sons Ltd.

 After 90 years as an International Harvester/Case IH dealer,  September 28 th 2019 was Chas Richards & Sons Ltd. 

last day as a Case IH dealer.  

We are proud to be the oldest family-owned and operated farm equipment dealer in York County, Ontario .

The decision to close has been a difficult one. 

It is because of our loyal customers that we have been able to continue to serve our community and support our families for 4 generations.

Any future announcements will be posted on our website.
