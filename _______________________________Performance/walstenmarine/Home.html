FAMILY OWNED & OPERATED SINCE 1972.
We are conveniently located right off of Highway 121, centrally located to Coboconk, Norland, Bobcaygeon, Minden, Haliburton and Fenelon Falls. We're a reasonable drive from Orillia, Bracebridge and the Muskokan hotspot of Huntsville.

Surrounded by and serving the lakes of Crystal, White, Salmon, Salerno, Four Mile, Silver, Shadow, Gull, Kashagawigamog, Kennisis, Balsam and Sturgeon to name a few. An exceptionally beautiful location to do your living, boating or boat shopping in. Come visit us in our showroom!

Brands we carry are Bennington Pontoon Boats, Campion, Monterey, Alumacraft and Yamaha Waverunners.
