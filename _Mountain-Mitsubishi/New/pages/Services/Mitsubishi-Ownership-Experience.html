MITSUBISHI OWNERSHIP EXPERIENCE

GET TO KNOW YOUR MITSUBISHI AND WE'LL GET TO KNOW YOU.

As a valued customer of Mitsubishi Motor Sales of Canada and our dealership, we want to ensure your ownership experience is a great one - every step of the way.
How do we do that? We’ve developed a program called MICAR to provide you with information and tools that help you get the most out of owning and maintaining Mitsubishi.

THIS IS WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT


At every service visit, our advisors will walk you through what you can expect during the appointment and present you with the factory required maintenance for your vehicle. Our work begins once you know what maintenance will be performed and we have your authorization.

 

THIS IS HOW WE KEEP YOU INFORMED

We realize that sometimes life can get busy and that maintenance may get overlooked. Through the MICAR program, your Mitsubishi dealer will notify you when it’s time to service your vehicle with a letter, email, text message or phone call. Of course, you can always book an appointment on our online scheduling page.

Schedule Your Service Appointment

THIS IS WHERE YOU CAN STAY ON TOP OF IT ALL

Register at MyMitsubishi.ca and gain access to your comprehensive owner’s website. This online portal gives you all of the information you need to care for, maintain and accessorize your Mitsubishi. You can also view your service history, identify your future maintenance requirements and tell us how you would like to receive communication from us.
