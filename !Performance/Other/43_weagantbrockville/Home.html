Welcome to

WEAGANT FARM SUPPLIES LTD.

Fred and Anita Weagant started the farm machinery business as a division of F.R.Weagant, General Merchant in South Mountain, Ontario. In 1953, Fred Weagant went to Smiths Falls to pick up a Hay Rake tongue and came home a Cockshutt dealer. Other manufacturers lines were added over time. The South Mountain dealership prospered and a new 6600 square foot was built on Hwy 43 in Winchester in 1976. The Winchester store sells a full line of New Holland tractors and equipment in addition to many other short lines of equipment.

In 1983, Weagant Farm Supplies expanded by opening a new location at 5077 Hwy 29, north of Brockville, Ontario. This location has a large inventory of Kubota tractors and equipment, Kawasaki ATVs and Mules, White Lawn tractors, and a large selection of used farm equipment. They can be visited electronically at www.weagantfarmbrockville.com

 

In 1993, Weagant Farm Supplies expanded into another style of business. Topline Trailer and Equipment Sales was created to offer a full line of horse, stock, cargo and landscape trailers to customers in Eastern Ontario and Western Quebec. Please visit Topline at 1226 County Road 31, north of the village of Winchester, ON. They can be visited electronically at www.toplinetrailers.com

Currently, a solid body of 63 employees excel in customer service. Second and third generation family members are continuing in the business started so many years ago by Fred and Anita Weagant. 2013 is our 60th Year anniversary.
