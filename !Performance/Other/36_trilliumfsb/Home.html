Company Overview

We service all clients, in any credit category, including self-employed with no proof of income, new immigrants with no credit history and those who have declared bankruptcy.

While our business is based in Ontario, we have provided our financial brokering services to customers throughout Canada. We are proud to have been recognized for our growth and business acumen as well as a leader in our field.

Trillium FSB has earned the honour of being the only financial services brokerage recommended by the Used Car Dealers Association (UCDA).
