Welcome to 
Twin Peaks Motorsports

Twin Peaks Motorsports is located in downtown Corner Brook on Newfoundland's West Coast. At Twin Peaks we pride ourselves on recognizing the needs of our customers and delivering outstanding products and service. As an authorized Honda and Yamaha dealer, we believe in providing the best quality products on the market, along with great customer service.

Whether you are in the Sales, Service, Parts, or Accessory Department, you will find that each member of our staff is eager to assist. Our enthusiasm for providing top quality powersports products at reasonable prices can be enjoyed in person or by browsing through our website for more product information. Contact our sales staff to schedule a road test on the bike of your dreams or to investigate our affordable financing options. We are here to get you riding!

To request more information on our snowmobiles, snow blowers, generators, clothing, etc., call (709) 634-5984 , email, or stop by our showroom at 12 Commercial Street in Corner Brook. We look forward to serving you!
