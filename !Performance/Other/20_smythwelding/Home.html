SMYTH WELDING & MACHINE SHOP LTD.
 

Smyth Welding and machine shop specializes in high quality snowblowers, land rollers, sweepers, stone windrowers, buckets and attachments and woodsplitters.

 

We are a manufacturer of Industrial as well as Residential Equipment. Some of our products include, Snow blowers, Sweepers, Land Rollers, and Stone Windrowers.

We are located close to beautiful Lake Huron in Auburn, Ontario Canada. Our company is owned and operated by Barry Smyth, and has been serving customer needs for over 55 years!

We are committed to building reliable, heavy duty equipment, and we stand behind our products, and are dedicated to making sure our customers are happy and completely satisfied with our products.

As mentioned above, we have been in business for over 55 years. Barry's father George began his love for welding at a very young age, and has ran a very successful business.

George retired in 1998, when Barry became the owner and carries on the tradition.

Our business has continued to diversify, prosper and grow. We employ approximately 21 skilled workers year round.

We supply many Dealers across Canada and the United States with our quality built products!

As we are the responsible producer of the tires we sell and import into Ontario we have partnered with YESS to manage our products’ end-of-life management.
Via YESS’s network management, we, the producer, pay for the collection, transportation and management to responsibly recycle our tires into new rubber products. To help you help us to recycle your tires at the end-of-life, please click  here to find a location nearest to you where you can drop off up to 10 tires off the rims at no charge.
