Welcome to ONeils Farm Equipment

 

Welcome and thank you for visiting O'Neil's farm and lawn equipment. O'Neil's takes pride in being a family owned business, which has served our community for over 100 years, since 1918. A lot of things have changed over the years, but the principles of our dealership has stayed the same. We believe in serving our customer with honesty and integrity.

We take great pride in finding our customers the right product for the right job. Our roots were founded in the agriculture business selling and servicing IH, CaseIH, and Case equipment. As our dealership has grown, we have expanded by taking on quality lawn and garden products.

We are proud to have partnered with quality companies such as Cub Cadet, Mahindra, Troy Bilt, Stihl , Grass Hopper, to name a few. With factory trained technicians, we are here to service all your needs. We can also offer our large fully equipped parts department to serve all your agriculture and lawn and garden needs as well. 

At O'Neil's we strive to offer our customers quality sales and service all under one roof. Once again thank you for visiting, we hope that our service that we provide builds relationships, not just a one time sale.

Yours Truly, Craig Smith.
