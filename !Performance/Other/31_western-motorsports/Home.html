Welcome to Western Motor Sports

We’ve come a long way from our beginnings as Snow & Water Sports Ltd, and with our new location at 68 Lundirgan Drive, we`re better suited to serve you than ever. Here in Corner Brook, NL, there isn't a friendlier or more knowledgeable staff than ours.

Western Motor Sports prides itself on being a full service recreation dealer. We carry such brands as Arctic Cat, Suzuki, Mercury, Lund and Larson with fully certified journeymen technicians on staff. So whether you are looking to purchase or service an existing snowmobile, ATV, outboard or boat, regardless of brand call us at (709) 634-3526 or email service@western-motorsports.ca.
