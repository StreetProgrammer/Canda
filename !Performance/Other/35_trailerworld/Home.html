Trailerworld Industries in BRANTFORD, ON is a full service trailer retailer, manufacturer and repair facility. On our premises we have a store, selling everything from axles to brakes, lights to couplers, wiring to jacks. We can provide your vehicle tow hitches and wiring harnesses, repair your damaged trailer and issue safety stickers, and direct you to the trailer that best suits your needs. We are the manufacturer of our own line of "Duratrail" galvanized trailers as well as "Crown" boat trailers.

Our experienced sales staff is eager to share its knowledge and enthusiasm with you. We encourage you to browse our online inventory, schedule a test drive and investigate financing options. You can also request more information about a trailer using our online form or by calling: (519) 752-1135 or Toll Free: 1 (888) 699-3313.

If you'd like a see a trailer in person, click on Dealership: Directions for step-by-step driving instructions to our site, or give us a call. We look forward to serving you. You can also email us from the Contact Us page.
