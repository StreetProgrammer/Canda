Coulter Heating & Air Conditioning

Coulter Heating and Air Conditioning is a fast-growing WaterFurnace geothermal dealer. We have been installing and servicing WaterFurnace geothermal systems for 12 years. We offer the complete installation including ductwork, furnace, and earthloop. We have factory-trained WaterFurnace technicians to service your existing WaterFurnace systems.

We offer the best warranties for a geothermal system in the industry and we are certified by the Canadian GeoExchange Coalition (CGC) to enable you to receive qualifying government rebates available on a geothermal system installation.
