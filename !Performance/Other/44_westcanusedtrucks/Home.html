Westcan Used Trucks Inc.

Your best source for used medium and heavy duty trucks in North America.
 

Westcan Used Trucks Inc. began operations in 1985. We are located in Innisfil, Ontario Canada. For 25 years the company has followed the principle of providing high-quality, pre-owned trucks to meet our customer needs. We carry a wide selection of all makes and models. Our inventory changes daily and we are always looking to buy quality pre-owned units so please give us a call today.

Servicing all of Canada and the USA, Westcan Used Trucks Inc. is your best source for used medium and heavy duty trucks in North America.
