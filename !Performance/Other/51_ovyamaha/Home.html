Welcome to 
Ottawa Valley Yamaha

Your Full Service Yamaha Dealer!
Welcome to Ottawa Valley Yamaha (formerly Banville's), your full service Yamaha and Stihl dealer located on Petawawa Blvd in Petawawa, Ontario.

Our showroom is filled with an excellent selection of brand new Yamaha ATVs, motorcycles, snowmobiles, and generators; Stihl lawn and garden equipment; and more! We also carry a selection of pre-owned and trade-in units from Yamaha and other manufacturers. And to top it all off we also have a wide variety of riding gear and accessories.

As an authorized Yamaha and Stihl dealer you can count on our factory trained technicians to take care of all your service needs. And for do-it-yourselfers our fully stocked parts department and friendly, knowledgeable staff will help you find the parts you need to get the job done right the first time. Difference"
