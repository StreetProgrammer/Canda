Welcome to
Mercury Service

Mercury Service Ltd. is a family-owned business started by Mr. Art McCulloch in 1946. The third generation of McCulloch's now work in all facets of the company, from the service to sales to accounting.

The company name changed to Mercury Service in 1959 when they started selling Mercury outboards. We became a Suzuki dealer in 1963 and a Yamaha dealer in 1986. Over the years we have sold many brands, such as Ski-Doo, Husqvarna, Lund, Martin, and Aeroliner. We NOW carry Yamaha (motorcycles, atvs, side by sides, Waverunners, snowmobiles, generators and outboards), Suzuki (motorcycles and atvs), Stingray fibreglass pleasure boats, Alumacraft aluminum boats & canoes, Manitou pontoon boats plus MB Sport wake boats.
