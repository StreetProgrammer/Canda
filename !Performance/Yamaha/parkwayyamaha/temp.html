Parkway Yamaha

Parkway Yamaha of Collingwood, is your premier motorcycle, ATV, watercraft/PWC and snowmobile dealership. We have been delivering trustworthy sales and service as Simcoe Countyâ€™s largest Yamaha dealer since 1979.

We provide parts and service to all make and models of recreational vehicles. We pride ourselves in offering the best options for our customers, including great deals on vehicles, parts and accessories, financing options for your new and used power sports vehicle and maintenance plans to keep you riding.

We carry a full line of new Yamaha motorcycles, dirt bikes, ATV's, snowmobiles, outboards, snowblowers, power washers and generators. We also carry a wide selection of pre-owned recreational vehicles of various makes and models. When something is not in stock, we have the experience to source and find the vehicle, part or accessory our customers want.

Our friendly, knowledgeable sales and service staff and convenient location have helped us maintain our reputation as the area's premier power sports dealer. Our service facility is staffed with technicians who care about your machine's performance and your safety.
