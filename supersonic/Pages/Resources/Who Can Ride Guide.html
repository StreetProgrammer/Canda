Non Riders - Beginners

You’ve never ridden a motorcycle? We'll get you rolling - you’ll learn braking, throttle control, cornering and shifting in that order. Whether you become a motorcyclist or not, your confidence will soar. Learn what riding a motorcycle is all about and why so many people enjoy it. If you have dreams to be a motorcycle racer, it all starts at Level .01 LEARN TO RIDE course.

If you are just starting out on two wheels and can operate all the controls of a motorcycle and want more practice and skills, look at our Level .02 CONQUER CORNERS course.

Street Riders

You always wanted to drag a knee in the corner? On the street is not the place to do this. At Super Sonic Road Race School we can provide all the technique in a step by step approach for you to experience it in a safe, low risk, environment. In the process, you will build your confidence and riding skills to better navigate the obstacles thrown at you on the street. We recommend to begin with our Level .03 LEARN TO LEAN course.

Roadracers

Super Sonic Road Race School offers you a place to learn and grow your road racing skills. Our Championship winning instructors are here teach you how to achieve your next level of speed. There are many courses to choose from or you can contact us to set-up a one on one clinic tailored just for you. We recommend to start at Level .04 ROAD RACE 101 Class

Flat Trackers

No secret that a lot of flat trackers became Road Racing Champions. For example, Canadian Michel Mercier, Americans Kenny Roberts and Nicky Hayden to name only a few. Come to Super Sonic School to try Road Racing, I bet you'll like it! We recommend to start with our Level .03 LEARN TO LEAN course. We will translate your flat track skills to Road Racing.

Off-Road Riders/Motocrossers/Trials Riders

Have you been riding off-road for a few years and Road Racing intrigues you? Are you’re thinking about getting your bike license? At the Super Sonic Road Race School you can try pavement riding in a low risk and low cost manner. We know you can ride a bike well, so we'll take you from your level of experience and translate your skills from dirt to pavement. Start at our Level .03 LEARN TO LEAN course.

Supermoto

Need work on your pavement game? Super Sonic Road Race School has the courses to help you learn breaking traction on pavement and backing it into the corner. If it’s just practice you want we can provide the tools you need. Send a message from the Contact Us button on the home page.
