Bud Robinson Motorsports

Located in Sault Ste. Marie Bud Robinson Motorsports is a locally owned and operated, family run business. We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service and knowledgeable staff. We want to make your buying experience exhilarating and hassle free.

We are a Kawasaki, Suzuki, Suzuki Marine and CF Moto dealer providing sales and service for Kawasaki and Suzuki Supersport, Street/Touring, Cruiser, Motocross, Dual Purpose, Off-Road, Mule, Side x Side, ATV, Teryx, and Jet Ski. Our Kawasaki factory trained technicians we service all makes and models of powersport vehicles. We service Harley Davidson, Yamaha, Suzuki, Honda, BMW, KTM,Triumph and many more. We stock a large variety of genuine Kawasaki,Suzuki & CF Moto parts and accessories along with after market parts, accessories and apparel.

Bud Robinson Motorsports believes in giving back to a sport that has been such a huge part of our lives. We are proud to sponsor Team Green riders Ryan Nolan, Seth Rogan, Dustin Greenwood and many others. Ryan has been teaching motocross schools and giving private lessons since 2003 and has taught in Canada, Bermuda, and all over the South Eastern United States .Ryan's experience comes from a lifetime of racing motocross, as well as training with and competing against some of the fastest motocross racers in the United States and Canada. Ryan has raced Canadian Outdoor Nationals, AMA Supercross, and Arenacross, and before turning pro, Ryan raced a number of years at the Canadian Amateur Nationals.
