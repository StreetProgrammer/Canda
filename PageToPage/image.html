GIBBONS MOTOR TOYS

Are you looking to spice up your summer on a jet boat? Have you been dreaming of creating a trail up the Rocky Mountains on a powerful snowmobile? How about that ATV and Side-By-Side that you wanted for so many years? Look no further, Gibbons Motor Toys, just 20 minutes north of Edmonton hosts all your dream machines from major manufacturers like Can-Am, Kingfisher, Coyote and Skidoo.

When you embark on a journey to our store in Gibbons, Alberta you are entering the world loved by fans of Edmonton motorsports. Is money the factor that holds you from buying your dream machine? Don't worry. We have the best financing plans in Edmonton too. So come for a visit. Our friendly staff will help you browse our entire inventory and set you up with a boat, ATV, side-by-side or the snowmobile you're interested in. We stock Can-Am, Kingfisher, Skidoo, Mercury and Marlon and more.
